04 / THE PHILOSOPHY

WHY THIS MATTERS

This website isn't just an example of neo-brutalism—it's a demonstration of modern collaboration. Human creativity provides vision, context, and judgment. AI provides execution speed, technical knowledge, and scalable expertise.

Neither could build this alone as effectively. The human might lack coding expertise or research time. The AI lacks creative vision and cannot define what "good" means for this project.

Together, we created something honest and functional—core brutalist values.

WHAT WE LEARNED

  • Iteration works: Each request refined the project incrementally
  • Clarity helps: Specific requests ("15 sources") produce better results
  • Trust the process: Human guides, AI executes, both learn
  • Be transparent: This page exists because collaboration deserves acknowledgment
  • Stay honest: AI wrote the code; human provided the vision—both matter
